  • (Jan 2024) A PhD position is available for 36 months between my research group at CNRS/University Grenoble Alpes (France) and Total Energies (France). The PhD student will be registered at Physics Doctoral School at University Grenoble Alpes where the work will be carried out. The PhD candidate will be cosupervised by Dr. Benoit Coasne (CNRS Researcher, LiPhy, https://benoitcoasne.github.io), Dr. Simon Gravelle (CNRS Researcher, LiPHY, https://simongravelle.github.io) and Dr. Yann Magnin (Total Energies, Research Center in Pau, France, https://www.yann-magnin.fr). The work deals with CO2 Capture by Nanoporous Materials: Molecular Simulation and Multiscale Approach (using thermodynamics and Lattice simulations) of adsorption and transport of molecules in nanoporous media. A more detailed presentation can be obtained upon request by email.
