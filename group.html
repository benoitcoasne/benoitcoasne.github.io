Job Offers

Feel free to send an email as we are always looking for candidates

  • (Fall 2023) A PhD position is available for 36 months between my research group at CNRS/University Grenoble Alpes (France) and IFP Energies Nouvelles (France). The PhD student will be registered at Physics Doctoral School at University Grenoble Alpes but the work will be carried out at IFP Energies Nouvelles under the supervision of Dr. Daniela Bauer. The work deals with the multiscale modeling (using thermodynamics and Lattice Boltzmann simulations) of adsorption and transport of complex molecules in nanoporous media. Funding is provided through IFP Energies Nouvelles. A more detailed presentation can be obtained upon request by email.

  • (Fall 2023) A postdoc position is available within my research group at CNRS/University Grenoble Alpes (France). The work deals with the modeling of adsorption and transport of dipolar and charged fluids in nanoporous media. This work is related to our recent work on "Adsorption and Confinement in Nanoporous Metallic Materials: Impact of Electronic Screening on Wetting and Phase Transitions" published in Nature Materials (2022). A more detailed presentation can be obtained upon request by email.
