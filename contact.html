Job Offers

Feel free to send an email as we are always looking for candidates

  • (Spring 2022) A joint postdoc position is available for 18 months between the University Grenoble Alpes (France) and the University of Rennes (France). This joint position, located in Grenoble, is in collaboration with Pr. Mikael Kepenekian. The work deals with the multiscale modeling (using both quantum and classical level simulations) of adsorption, diffusion or reaction of small gases at the surface of carbide/nitride materials. Funding is provided through the French national agency (ANR, research project MENIHR led by F. Tessier). A more detailed presentation can be obtained upon request by email.

  • (Fall 2022) A PhD position is available  at University Grenoble Alpes (France) on  molecular modeling of acoustic response of fluid adsorption and transport in nanoporous media. Funding is provided by the French National Agency. Please send email for any enquiry regarding this project.
